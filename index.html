The year is 3062; it has been ten years since the Clan Invasion of the Inner Sphere and tensions are building once again. The Federated Commonwealth is on the verge of civil war, the Draconis Combine launches a renewed offensive against Ghost Bear. Elsewhere, the Word of Blake grows in strength.

On the game world of Solaris however, despite nationalist riots, the tournaments continue. This glittering play world is the home of the great Solaris Games, where thousands flock to watch BattleMechs beat the living hell out of each other for their entertainment.

These gladiatorial matches between the titans of the modern battlefield are the centre of attention; MechWarriors, whether down on their luck or just itching for some action and money, are drawn like moths to a flame, as are fans and tourists from across the Inner Sphere.

Fortunes are won and lost, reputations built on fame or infamy, and for many it can become a way of life.

What you'll find here are the rules, leader boards, and bios of all the participants from the various seasons we intend to have. As for how the battles will be partaken, if you are local to your opponent, you can opt for good old table top. If not, we recommend using MegaMek (currently recommend 0.48).

Be sure to check out the rules to see how we are running this and check back often as our first season begins soon.
