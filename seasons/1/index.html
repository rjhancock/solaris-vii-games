Thank you so much to everybody who's signed up for this first season of the MegaMek Invitational!

All matches MUST be played using the MM rules preset XML and should be completed by Sunday, June 27th, 2021.

The official map pack for this season can be downloaded here and should be unzipped and placed in the data/boards/MegaMek Invitational directory for ease of sorting.

Seeds have completed and the results can be found on the main Seeds page. Keep tabs on this page for updates as things move forward in the first ever MegaMek Invitational on Solaris VII!
